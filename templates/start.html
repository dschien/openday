How much energy does visiting The Guardian website consume?

It depends on your device, they way you connect and how much time you spent in the site. We've prepared an application for you to explore these options.

We'd like you to take a quick survey. Your participation in the survey is entirely voluntary.

Privacy Statement:

All responses are anonymous and no personally identifiable information is requested in this survey. Consequently there will be no way to retrieve specific individual responses once the survey is complete.

The data collected will be analysed at the University of Bristol and relevant results shared both within the academic community and with the Guardian News Group.

None of the data collected will be shared with any other third party.
